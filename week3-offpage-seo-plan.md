# Week 3 Off-Page SEO Plan (NOVA BODA)

## Goal
Increase authority for Valencia wedding queries through local signals, backlinks, and review growth.

## Priority keywords to support
- proveedores de boda en valencia
- fincas para bodas en valencia
- fotografo de boda valencia
- catering boda valencia
- dj boda valencia

## 1) Local citations (Days 1-2)

- Google Business Profile
- Bing Places
- Apple Business Connect
- Facebook Page
- Instagram business profile link

- Valencia wedding/event directories

Rules:
- Use exactly the same business name and website URL in all profiles.
- Add the same short description adapted per platform.
- Include primary category and 2-3 secondary categories.

## 2) Backlink outreach (Days 2-4)
Target 20-30 relevant sites:
- Wedding photographers in Valencia
- Fincas / venues
- Florists and catering partners
- Bridal blogs and local magazines
- Event planners and stylists

Offer one of these linkable assets:
- Practical guide pages (already published)
- Co-branded checklist PDF
- "Best wedding planning resources in Valencia" roundup collaboration

KPIs:
- 20 outreach emails sent
- 5 positive replies
- 2-4 new backlinks in first cycle

## 3) Reviews and trust signals (Days 3-5)
Collect first-party testimonials and external reviews:
- Ask couples/providers for specific feedback (service + result + city)
- Request Google reviews with direct short link
- Reuse best testimonials on key pages

Target:
- +10 new Google reviews in 30 days
- Average rating >= 4.8

## 4) Content distribution (Days 4-6)
Promote each guide page on:
- Instagram (carousel + CTA to guide)
- Stories with poll/FAQ
- WhatsApp broadcast to partners
- LinkedIn post for B2B provider audience

Repurpose each guide into:
- 1 short video script
- 1 carousel
- 3 short posts

## 5) Tracking and iteration (Day 7)
Review:
- New backlinks
- Impressions/clicks in Search Console
- Branded searches
- Referral traffic by source

Double down next week on channels with highest CTR and partner response rate.
